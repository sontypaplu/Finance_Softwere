PHASE 4 — REAL CALCULATED REMAINING PAGES

This pack extends the previous live/calculated phases by replacing more seed-heavy terminal pages with SQLite-backed calculated route logic where data is available, while preserving fallback seeds for missing session/provider/data conditions.

Included route upgrades:
- /api/terminal/risk
- /api/terminal/planning
- /api/terminal/rebalancing
- /api/terminal/reports

Service upgrades:
- lib/services/terminal-data-service.ts
- lib/services/report-service.ts

Notes:
- Risk now derives allocation, sector exposure, factor exposure, correlation matrix, and scenario impacts from current holdings/history where available.
- Planning now uses real income-center derived cashflow and liabilities, with goals remaining modeled placeholders until a real goal model is added.
- Rebalancing now derives current sleeve weights, target drifts, and suggested trade actions from live holdings.
- Reports now reads report rows from the database when available and falls back to demo report seeds otherwise.
- This is a real calculated-data phase, but still not the final institutional-grade engine.
